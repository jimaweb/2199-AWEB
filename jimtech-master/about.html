History

JimTech Electronics Inc. was founded in 2023 by our Chief Executive Officer Jimwel L. Valdez. He envisioned a bright and hopeful future by utilizing the current technologies for the better, not for worse. Since the beginning, the company has done its best efforts to learn, innovate, and ensure productivity of clients through their product offerings. It has already received awards just in its 1st year. It was the 3rd Best Start-up Company 2023 from Philippine Kinabukasan Awards. The company also received two (2) Gold Medals from the Asia Tech Revolution Awards held in Singapore at the same year.
